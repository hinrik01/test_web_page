About us

Ship O'Cereal is a company founded by a great man by the name of Arnar Leifsson. Arnar entrusted his website in to the hands of students currently studying at the University of Reykjavík. This is group 41's implementation for this project. The group worked tirelessly on the website to have functionality as well as a beautiful design.

Group 41 is composed of four members, three of which are studying computer science and one that is studying software engineering. The members are Andrea Einarsdóttir, Atli Karl Sigurbjartsson, Hermann Guðmundsson and Hinrik Pétur Jóhannsson.
